The packages I maintain

I decided to make them easier to find and created labels for them on Debian Salsa (A GitLab instance that hosts the packages and CIs):

  • @wdes-packaging-team
  • @phpmyadmin-packaging-team

Libraries

  • tcpdf, I adopted it in October 2019 and have been maintaining the packaging and sending it to stable-backports.
  • jquery-timepicker, I adopted it in January 2021 and it's been a pleasure to package it and add tests [git].
  • php-code-lts-u2f-php-server used by phpMyAdmin for legacy U2F, it's a library I took back maintenance at @code-lts.
  • mariadb-mysql-kbs, my data library I made in July 2018. Maintained up to date by my nice bot.
  • dasprid-enum
  • baconqrcode
  • php-zumba-json-serializer
  • php-slim-psr7
  • google-recaptcha

phpMyAdmin libraires

  • twig-i18n-extension previously twig-extensions
  • phpmyadmin-shapefile
  • phpmyadmin-motranslator
  • phpmyadmin-sql-parser

Tools

  • phpmyadmin, the phpMyAdmin package.
  • phpsysinfo, I adopted it in January 2023 to save it from beeing missing from Debian bookworm !
  • phpldapadmin, I saved/adopted it and made PHP 8.1/8.2 support.
  • shunit2, I finished the hard refurubishing work and got it updated in Debian for bookworm.
  • phing, I adopted it to save it from beeing missing from Debian bookworm !
